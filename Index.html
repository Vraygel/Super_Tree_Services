Services

Tree Removal

Florida's unstable weather is a danger to your homes and trees. As experts, we know how to carefully and safely remove trees without harming your property. We remove trees with specialized equipment. Trust the professionals

Tree Trimming

If you need to remove obstructive tree branches in your yard, our professionals and special equipment will help you in this. We will do everything qualitatively and quickly.

Stumd Grinding

There are few things that are more jarring than an old stump in an otherwise pristine lawn. Whether it was left from another company or from a DIY tree removal, we here at Tree Care America can take that stump out and leave the lawn cleaner than when we got there.

Site Clearance

Largescale site clearance is often required to enable new developments to take place.

Your sites can be cleared of trees and vegetation with specialist equipment such as a mulcher, flail, shredder and mechanical stump grinders, along with specialist pruning/felling undertaken by fully qualified tree surgeons who are experienced in problematic or difficult site clearance situations.

Land Cleaning

Land clearing is the first step in a construction project. The experts will prepare the land by clearing and grading it thoroughly.

Crass mowing

Grass mowing must be correctly done. The height of cut is important; it should not be so low that the grass is shaved to ground level, brown patches appear and moss and other unwanted plants move in; and it should not be so high that the grass turns brown at the base, while remaining green at the tips.

Professionals will do it for you.
